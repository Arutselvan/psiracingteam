About

Welcome to
PSI Racing Team

A group of self-motivated automobile enthusiasts who strive to push the limits of engineering to create the perfect all-terrain vehicle. PSI Racing had a humble start to the all-terrain racing scenario back in 2008, when PSI was still nascent. As years went by, we grew from a mere competitior to a stern pioneer in the field of BAJA racing. The legacy of PSI Racing is still going strong with the latest horse from our stables, Omega 91. Being a centrally funded technical institution based in the historical city of Tiruchirappalli, we aim to incorporate our engineering and cultural aspects into our design. Our success over the years, have transformed us into a feared competitor among the top teams of India.

Achievements

PSI Racing has been a constant participant in nation-wide events such as BAJA SAE India and Enduro Student India (formerly known as Student BAJA India).

Our achievements:

- 2nd position,Suspension & Traction, BAJA SAE India 2016
- 2nd position Overall, 1st position in Traction, 2nd position in Sales Presentation, BAJA Student India, 2016
- Safest Car Award, BAJA SAE India, 2014
- 2nd in Tamil Nadu Overall, 2013
- 1st in Hill Climb, 2009
- Our journey began, 2006

Vision

PSI Racing aims to expand its horizons by venturing into new genres of racing such as Formula Racing and also grow on an international scale in the BAJA scene soon. Our vision has been focussed to extract the maximum out of the limited resources that we currently possess. We envision goals realistically, approach the problem amicably and come out with flying colours ecstatically. Here's to our 'Racing Our Dreams'.
